Open University

I began a module with the Open University in September 2016, in English Literature. The module was called The Arts, Past and Present. I achieved a pass in this module. I enjoyed this module, and although the initial plan was to continue the full course over the course of 6 years, I realised half way through that I wasn't actually sure I would end up using the degree once I had finished, and was more eager to get into work full time again rather than spending the following 5 years as a student. My interest in Web Development remained for the entirety of this year, with me deciding I would persue a career in this once I had completed this year at University.

The year at the Open University was very beneficial for me. This is because it engaged my brain again, and helped me re-learn and improve upon my time management skills. I remained a month ahead for the majority of the year, and received good marks and feedbacks from my tutor.
